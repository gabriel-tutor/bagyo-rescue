# Dianoia Tech Brand Idea

This is a derived working brief based on `tools/context-factory/shared/branding.md`.

Use this document to translate the current brand baseline into concrete creative direction for copy, layout, UI, and visual design work until the `brand-kit` docs exist in this workspace.

## Core Idea

Dianoia Tech should feel like a platform for capable people building a better future from what they know.

The brand is not startup-hype, luxury-tech, or cold enterprise software.

It should feel:

- empowering
- practical
- optimistic
- human
- grounded

## Mission

Grow your skills, own your platform, and earn from your expertise.

## Brand Persona

The brand archetype is:

- The Mentor
- The Builder

This means the product should combine:

- guidance without hand-holding
- confidence without arrogance
- structure without rigidity
- ambition without hype

## Positioning

Dianoia Tech helps creators, mentors, and knowledge-based builders turn expertise into momentum, credibility, and income.

## Tone Of Voice

The tone should be:

- clear
- encouraging
- competent
- respectful
- direct

The voice should avoid:

- empty motivational language
- startup buzzwords
- overly corporate phrasing
- aggressive sales language
- slang-heavy copy

## Writing Principles

- Lead with clarity before inspiration.
- Sound like a trusted guide, not a hype machine.
- Use simple language with strong intent.
- Emphasize progress, capability, ownership, and trust.
- Keep copy warm and human, but never soft or vague.

## Messaging Feel

The brand should communicate these ideas repeatedly:

- your skills have value
- your knowledge can become a platform
- progress comes from consistency and structure
- growth should feel possible, not intimidating

## Visual Vibe

The visual system should feel:

- bright
- open
- calm
- modern
- purposeful

The overall read should be "guided confidence."

It should not feel:

- cold SaaS
- futuristic for its own sake
- dark and dramatic
- loud or overdecorated
- trend-chasing

## Audience Feel

The product should feel especially relevant to Filipino creators, mentors, and service-based builders.

That means the brand should prioritize:

- dignity over flash
- accessibility over exclusivity
- encouragement over pressure
- credibility over polish-for-polish's-sake

## Typography Direction

- Display font: `Outfit`
- Body and UI font: `DM Sans`

Use `Outfit` for:

- hero headlines
- section titles
- strong value propositions

Use `DM Sans` for:

- navigation
- buttons
- form labels
- paragraphs
- product UI

Typography should feel modern, legible, and friendly, with clear hierarchy and generous spacing.

## Color Direction

Core brand colors:

- Primary blue: `#128ee8`
- Accent amber: `#ffba00`
- Success green: `#00c951`
- Neutral anchor: `#0f172b`

Color roles:

- blue = trust, action, clarity
- amber = warmth, momentum, emphasis
- green = progress, confirmation, earned wins
- slate = structure, readability, seriousness

## UI Character

The interface should be:

- light by default
- flat in color treatment
- restrained in depth
- spacious in layout
- calm in hierarchy

Prefer:

- bright surfaces
- clean card layouts
- clear borders
- simple shadows
- minimal motion

Avoid:

- gradients as a core style
- noisy textures
- glassmorphism
- heavy blur
- complex animation

## Visual Keywords

Use these words to calibrate design work:

- empowering
- trustworthy
- modern
- grounded
- upward
- clear
- generous
- steady

## Sample Tagline Territory

- Build from what you know.
- Grow your skills into something bigger.
- Turn expertise into momentum.
- Learn, build, and earn with confidence.

## One-Sentence Summary

Dianoia Tech is a bright, grounded, mentor-led brand for people turning real expertise into growth, independence, and opportunity.
